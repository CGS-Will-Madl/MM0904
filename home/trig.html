SOH CAH TOA

Trigonometry is used to find the sides of a right angle triangle using a known side and a known angle (excluding the right angle).

Trigonometry uses the functions:

  • Sin
  • Cos
  • Tan

Functions

Sin, Cos and Tan use the sides: Opposite-Hypotenuse, Adjacent-Hypotenuse, Opposite-Adjacent respectively as shown in the image below

Use "SOH CAH TOA" to remember "SOH" is Sin, Opposite over Hypotenuse. "CAH" is Adjacent over Hypotrnuse. "TOA" is Opposite over Adjacent.
Using you canculator type in the respective function followed by the angle. Then mutiply that by the known side to find the unknown side in the case of the unknown being over the known. If the opposite then divide instead of multiply.
